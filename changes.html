Some more tool models

Fixed NPCs going into T-pose

Half-Life 2: Deathmatch was overriding the animations from other games. All Half-Life NPC animations will now load, including those Episode 1 and Episode 2, even if you do not have them mounted.

Counter-Strike: Source weapons

You do not need to have CS:S mounted anymore to use or see their models! They now ship as part of Garry's Mod, because they were improved to integrate far better with our player animations. If you do not have CS:S mounted, however, very low resolution textures will be used instead (a system that has been in place for a few updates now).

Player model updates

Some newer systems used by Valve were retrofitted onto Garry's Mod's animations, so they now blend together much more naturally. Some more animations have been added, such as crouch-walking as a zombie. The knife and dual-wielding animations were updated - but that's barely scratching the surface, as many other improvements were made too. And last but not least, all player models are now accessible by everyone, regardless of which games are mounted!
